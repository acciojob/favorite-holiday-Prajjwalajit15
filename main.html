Manali - The Gem of Himachal Pradesh


Nestled in the Kullu Valley of Himachal Pradesh, Manali is a breathtaking holiday destination surrounded by snow-capped mountains, lush green valleys, and pristine rivers. It offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and adventure, making it a favorite among travelers.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Solang Valley: Experience thrilling activities like paragliding and skiing amidst the stunning landscape.
  2. Hidimba Devi Temple: Pay homage at this ancient temple built in dedication to Hidimba Devi, a character from the Mahabharata.
  3. Rohtang Pass: Enjoy panoramic views of the Himalayas and indulge in snow adventures during the winter months.
  4. Manu Temple: Visit the temple dedicated to Sage Manu, who is considered the creator of human race in Hindu mythology.
  5. Old Manali: Explore the charming streets, cafes, and unique shops of this bohemian neighborhood.

Must-Try Foods

  • Chana Madra: A traditional Himachali dish made with chickpeas and a rich yogurt-based gravy.
  • Thukpa: A Tibetan noodle soup that provides warmth and comfort, perfect for the chilly weather.
  • Sidu: Wheat flour bread filled with local herbs and spices, often served with ghee.
  • Apricot Jam: Sample the delicious locally-made apricot jam, a sweet souvenir of Manali.
  • Patande: Himachali pancakes made from wheat flour, a delightful breakfast option.
